## Overview

Prolimpa is a **distributor of cleaning products and industrial stationery**, servicing businesses and facilities in Nogales. Their primary goal was to showcase their catalog and allow procurement teams to browse and reach out for orders efficiently.

## Strategy

We created a digital catalog site that functions as a **B2B acquisition funnel**, focused on clarity, product grouping, and direct channels of communication with the team.

## Key Elements

- Product sections organized by category and demand
- Emphasis on industrial applications and bulk purchasing
- Quote/request form and WhatsApp buttons for fast orders
- Brand-aligned design to reflect reliability and consistency
