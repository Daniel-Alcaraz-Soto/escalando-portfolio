## Overview

ZB Mantenimiento rents industrial machinery and equipment in Nogales. The goal was to build an online presence that immediately converts traffic into business leads via quote requests.

## Strategy

We developed a lightweight landing site focused on local visibility and service clarity, structured around quote generation and WhatsApp communication.

## Key Elements

- Direct call-to-action to quote requests and WhatsApp contact
- Clear service breakdown with visual support
- Local SEO strategy to appear on relevant search queries
- Responsive design for on-the-go decision makers

## Outcome

Thanks to their online presence, **ZB Mantenimiento receives continuous sales and inquiries** from local businesses. They’ve secured a leading position in the Nogales machinery rental space without needing heavy advertising.
