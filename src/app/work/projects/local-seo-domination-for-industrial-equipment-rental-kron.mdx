## Overview

KRON offers industrial equipment rental services in Nogales, Sonora. Their challenge was to appear at the top of local search results without relying on paid media.

## Strategy

We designed a lean, conversion-oriented website optimized around high-volume local keywords and service-based intent. Instead of relying on ads, KRON invested in **pure SEO** — and the results speak for themselves.

## Key Elements

- Keyword-focused content structure
- On-page SEO with schema, metadata, and clean URLs
- Performance-optimized layout with clear CTA to request quotes
- Responsive, mobile-first design

## Outcome

KRON became the **#1 ranked local equipment rental provider in Nogales**, gaining ongoing quote requests and dominating search visibility in their industry — all without paid advertising.
