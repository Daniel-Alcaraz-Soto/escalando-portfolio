## Overview

White Dentistry specializes in cosmetic dental treatments, including **veneers and implants**. They needed a high-converting digital presence to attract high-ticket leads actively searching for these procedures in their city.

## Strategy

We developed a streamlined landing page focused on **trust, transformation, and immediacy**. The page included before-and-after visuals, doctor credentials, and a direct phone CTA to convert intent into action.

## Paid Traffic

Google Ads campaigns were launched targeting specific queries around:
- "Veneers in Nogales Sonora"
- "Dental implants affordable"
- "Smile design clinic near me"

Ads drove traffic directly to the landing page and phone number — bypassing complex lead forms to maximize real-time inquiries.

## Key Elements

- Fast, mobile-first landing page
- Local SEO + ad relevance alignment
- Google Ads campaign structure focused on transactional intent
- Phone-first CTA for high-conversion rate

## Outcome

The funnel generated **consistent calls and inquiries** from potential patients searching for high-value dental services. White Dentistry now has a scalable traffic + conversion machine built specifically for their niche.
